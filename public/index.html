Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoHireBot

How fast are matches delivered?
+
Our AI matches healthcare professionals and job openings in under 2 minutes, sending real-time email notifications to both parties immediately upon finding a match.
Is my personal data safe?
+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted end-to-end and we are fully HIPAA compliant. Your information is never sold to third parties, and you can request deletion at any time.
How does recruiter verification work?
+
Upon registration, recruiters receive an alphanumeric OTP via email. This verification ensures only legitimate healthcare facilities can post job openings and access candidate information.
What specialties are supported?
+
We support all major nursing and healthcare specialties including ICU/Critical Care, Emergency Room, Medical-Surgical, Pediatrics, Labor & Delivery, Operating Room, Oncology, Cardiology, Geriatrics, and Psychiatric nursing.
Is AutoHireBot really free?
+
Yes! The platform is currently free for all healthcare job seekers. Recruiters may have access to premium features in the future, but core matching will always remain accessible.
