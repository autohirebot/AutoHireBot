Frequently Asked Questions

What is the starting salary for nurses in Hyderabad?
Fresher nurses with GNM can expect ₹18,000-₹25,000 per month, while BSc Nursing graduates start at ₹22,000-₹30,000 in private hospitals. Government hospitals offer higher starting salaries of ₹35,000+.
Which hospital pays the highest salary in Hyderabad?
Government hospitals like NIMS and Osmania offer the highest salaries (₹38,000-₹70,000). Among private hospitals, Apollo and Continental offer competitive packages of ₹30,000-₹55,000.
Is nursing council registration from other states valid in Hyderabad?
Yes, but you need to get it transferred to Telangana State Nursing Council within 3 months of joining. Some hospitals accept out-of-state registration initially.
