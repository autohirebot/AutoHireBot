AIIMS Nursing Recruitment 2026 Overview

AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences) is India's most prestigious healthcare institution. Every year, AIIMS recruits thousands of nursing officers across 20+ institutes. This is your complete guide to AIIMS nursing recruitment 2026.

📋 Quick Facts

  • Post: Nursing Officer (Staff Nurse Grade-II)
  • Vacancies: 3,000+ expected
  • Pay Scale: Level 7 (₹44,900 - ₹1,42,400)
  • Age: 18-30 years
  • Qualification: BSc Nursing only
